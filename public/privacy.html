Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026 · Applies to: DocuGen Pro (monday.com app)

Nexlabs ("we", "us", or "our") built DocuGen Pro as a commercial app for the monday.com marketplace. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who We Are

Nexlabs is the developer of DocuGen Pro. Contact us at support@nexlabs.online.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Your monday.com account ID and user ID.
  • Board data: Board IDs, item names, and column values you use for document generation.
  • Document templates: The .docx files you upload.
  • Signature data: Signer names, emails, and signature status.
  • App settings: Language, timezone, company data, notification email.
  • Usage data: Timestamps of document generation events for billing.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver documents you request.
  • To send e-signature requests to signers you specify.
  • To notify you when documents are signed.
  • To enforce plan limits and calculate billing usage.
  • To improve reliability and performance of our service.

We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on secure servers hosted on Railway (United States). We use AES-256 encryption for OAuth tokens, HTTPS for all data in transit, and enforce HSTS on all endpoints.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

If you uninstall DocuGen Pro, all data associated with your account is deleted within 10 days. You may request immediate deletion by emailing support@nexlabs.online.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Railway — server hosting (United States)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • monday.com — platform integration

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Email support@nexlabs.online. We respond within 30 days.
